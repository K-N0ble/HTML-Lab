Background

I grew up in Airdrie, Alberta, a smaller city just outside of Calgary. I attented the same school from kindergarten all the way until I graduated Grade 12.
Ever since I was little, my family would regularly take trips to the mountains, and that is where I found a love for snowboarding.
I also have loved many different video games throughout my childhood, which I think largely contributed to my interest in technology and art.

Hobbies

These are some of my hobbies currently!:

  • Games
  • Digital and traditional art
  • Snowboarding

My Workplace

I began working at London Drugs in August of 2023 in the tech department. I have since also recieved training in the PhotoLab,
where I get to work with printers, edit pictures, print canvases, and produce a variety of different gifting items.
I really enjoy working here because it lets me explore my interests and my co-workers have been great!

Future

I am currently learning programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Swift.
I hope to further my education in all of these to eventually build personal projects to help develop my portfolio.
